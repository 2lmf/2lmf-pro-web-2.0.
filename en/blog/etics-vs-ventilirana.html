Facade: Classic (ETICS) or Ventilated? Guide for a smart choice

PUBLISHED: JANUARY 11, 2026 | AUTHOR: 2LMF PRO

When the time comes to restore a facade or build a new facility, most clients find themselves in a dilemma: choose a proven "classic" facade (ETICS) or invest in a modern ventilated facade?

At 2LMF PRO we offer systems for both types of facades. To make your decision easier, we have prepared a detailed comparison of benefits and costs.

1. ETICS (Contact Facade) – The Queen of Price/Quality Ratio

This is the system you see on 90% of family houses. It consists of insulation boards (rock wool or EPS) that are glued directly to the wall, reinforced with mesh and finished with decorative plaster.

  • Key benefits: Excellent thermal insulation in winter, monolithic system without interruptions, fast execution and unlimited choice of colors.
  • Price: Financially the most favorable option ("Best Buy"). Ideal for standard residential buildings.

2. Ventilated Facade – The "Mercedes" among Facades

This system is considered a premium solution. The key difference is in the air layer between the insulation and the final cladding (HPL, fiber cement, aluminum).

  • Key benefits:
    • Summer comfort: The chimney effect carries away the heat, the interior remains cool without air conditioning.
    • Building health: Continuous ventilation prevents moisture and mold.
    • Durability: Final panels are resistant to extreme weather conditions and impacts.
  • Price: Higher initial investment, but zero maintenance costs for the next 50 years.

Conclusion

Planning a facade? The key is to choose a system that suits your long-term goals and budget. 2LMF PRO team is at your disposal for expert advice and precise material calculation.
