Want a Web Shop, but Fiscalization scares you? Here is a solution from 0€ per month.

PUBLISHED: FEBRUARY 3, 2026 | AUTHOR: SHARPSHARK DIGITAL

Introduction: The myth of an expensive Web Shop

Many small entrepreneurs give up on selling online because they think they need expensive equipment, complicated developers and monthly payments for "fiscal cash registers".

The truth is different. If you sell wine, cheese, oil or handicrafts, the law allows you a simple and cheap way of working, only if you know how to set up the process.

The Problem: "Why is it so complicated?"

If you work with cash, you must have a fiscal cash register at the moment of sale. But, with a Web Shop, most customers pay transactionally (by payment to account, internet banking).

Here we come to the key difference: **Transactional account = Fiscalization not required at the moment of click.** The invoice can be issued subsequently, after you see the payment.

The Solution: Model "Order -> Quote -> Invoice"

Instead of paying for expensive scripts that automatically fiscalize every detail, we propose a model used by smart small businesses:

  1. Web Shop as "Catalog": The customer adds products to the cart and clicks "Finish order".
  2. The system sends a "Quote": The customer automatically receives a Quote on email with all payment details and a 2D barcode.
  3. Payment: The customer scans the code and pays by phone.
  4. Issuing an Invoice: You see the payment and through free services send a fiscalized invoice with one click.

Why is this better?

  • Cost 0 €/month: You don't need to pay monthly rent for a "web cash register".
  • Fewer errors: You have full control over the invoice issuance process.
  • 100% Legal: The model is fully compliant with Croatian laws.
